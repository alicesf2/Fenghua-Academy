Background

Fenghua Academy is located in the northwest suburb of Chicago, Lake Zurich, Illinois. It is a non-profit 501 3(c) educational organization approved by the IRS. The school was found by several community volunteers in the year 2004. Dr. Ron Li serves as the principal. The school has grown to over 300 students in 2008 from its opening in 2004.

Opportunity

We offer Chinese language, math, music, art, dance, martial arts, college prep classes and other varieties of extracurricular activities. We have a strong team of over 30 qualified teachers and an excellent team of community leaders and enthusiastic volunteers. We also offer a Culture Exchange Summer Program for local high school students, working with schools in China.

Culture

Fenghua Chinese school is a hub for international culture enrichment, volunteering, and leadership development. Our school is the center of the northwest suburb community where parents and children can come together to learn, socialize and have fun. We serve children of all ages regardless of their race. We also have classes for adults and offer free ESL classes for senior citizens. We are the number one Chinese school in the Ela and Vernon area.

Mission

The mission of Fenghua Academy is to promote "Friends, Growth and Community." Our purpose is to help students develop a stronger foundation in academics, social skills, and responsibilities and to unify our community.
