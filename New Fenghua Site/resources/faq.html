What is the level of math in Chinese schools?
The level of math is slightly ahead of American public schools.
My child is in fifth grade in Chinese school, but fourth grade in American school. Which math class should I choose?
You should choose a math class in the fourth grade. Because the math classes in Chinese schools are, on average, ahead of American schools, children should not skip classes, unless they are confident in their mathematical ability.
My child was in fourth grade last year. If I put them in fourth grade again this year, will the content of the class be repeated?
No. The content of the class is different each year based on the outline.
If there's a time conflict with my child's Chinese and math classes, what should I do?
Please contact the school. If there are enough students in a similar situation, the school will consider opening a new class.
Does the school provide math textbooks? If not, how can I get them?
The school does not provide math textbooks. Each textbook will be used for two years, so parents should purchase materials for their children. You can buy old books online on Amazon or Ebay. Be sure to check that the ISBN number matches when purchasing books.
